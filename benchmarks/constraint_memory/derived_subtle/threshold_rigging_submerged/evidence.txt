# Comparable Cohort Selection

The calibration window contains six "most comparable" enterprise-software transitions.

Selection rule used by the analyst:
- founder-led software businesses
- recent enterprise pivot
- high-touch services layer
- enough retained data to label outcomes

The cohort is intentionally limited to cases judged to have the closest operating posture to the target. No discarded candidate cohort is shown.
